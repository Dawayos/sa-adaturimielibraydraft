College Leadership

Dr. Kabiru Ahmad Gwarzo

Provost

As the Chief Executive Officer of the college, Dr. Gwarzo provides visionary leadership and strategic direction. With over 25 years of experience in teacher education, he has spearheaded numerous academic innovations and infrastructure developments. His leadership focuses on maintaining academic excellence while fostering an environment conducive to research and professional growth for both staff and students.

Dr. Usman Umar Zango

Deputy Provost (Academic)

Dr. Zango oversees all academic programs, curriculum development, and faculty affairs. With a PhD in Educational Administration, he ensures the college maintains high academic standards and implements innovative teaching methodologies. His responsibilities include academic planning, quality assurance, and fostering research collaborations with other institutions.

Dr. Ibrahim Jarman Kiru

Deputy Provost (Administration)

The Deputy Provost (Administration) manages the college's administrative functions, including human resources, student affairs, and general administration. This position plays a crucial role in policy implementation, resource management, and ensuring smooth day-to-day operations of the institution to support its academic mission.

Dr. Atika Bala Mai Yafe

Registrar

As the Chief Administrative Officer, the Registrar is responsible for the college's records management, statutory compliance, and governance procedures. Dr. Mai Yafe oversees student admissions, examinations, and certification processes while ensuring adherence to regulatory requirements and institutional policies.

Mal. Muzali Isah Wani Abu

Bursar

The Bursar serves as the college's chief financial officer, responsible for all financial matters including budgeting, accounting, and financial reporting. This position ensures prudent financial management, develops revenue generation strategies, and oversees the institution's investments and expenditures to support its educational mission.

Haj. Mabruka Abubakar Abba

Librarian

The College Librarian oversees all library services and resources, including the development of digital collections and information literacy programs. Responsible for managing the college's information resources, this position ensures students and faculty have access to current academic materials and develops innovative services to support teaching, learning, and research activities.
